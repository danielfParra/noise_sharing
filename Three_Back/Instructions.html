Welcome to the {{ n_back }}-Back Task!

In this game, you will see a series of numbers appearing one after the other. The sequence consists of {{ length }} numbers in total.

Your job is to carefully watch the sequence and press the space bar only when the current number is exactly the same as the number that appeared {{ n_back }} steps earlier. You have time to react until the number disappears after {{ trial_duration }} seconds.

Pressing the space bar at the wrong time or failing to press it when needed both count as errors. If you make an error, ✗ will appear, and if your response is correct, ✓ will show.


How to Play

  • For each new number, remember the number that appeared {{ n_back }} items ago.
  • If the current number is the same as the number {{ n_back }} items before, press the space bar.
  • If it is not the same, do not press anything.


Scoring System

You start with {{ starting_points }} points, which we will later convert into pesos.

  • If you miss a target (not pressing the space bar when you should), you lose {{ penalty_miss }} points.
  • If you press the space bar by mistake (when the current number does not match {{ n_back }} items ago), you lose {{ penalty_wrong_press }} points.
  • Important: Pressing the space bar for every number or never pressing at all will both lead to losing {{ penalty_spam }} points. Try to be careful and only press when you are sure!

What to Aim For

  • Be attentive: Watch each number carefully.
  • Make your decision: Only press the space bar when you notice a match.
  • Keep your points: Your goal is to finish the game with as many points as possible.

Try your best, and good luck!
